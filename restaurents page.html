    "Culinary Excellence Awaits: Delight Your Senses at our restaurants,
    Where Every Dish is a Masterpiece"

    Radisson Blu Resort, Galle hosts four on-site restaurants and a rooftop bar, serving a range of international cuisines, breakfast buffets, and refreshing drinks. Enjoy a delicious dinner at Fusion overlooking the pool and terrace area or head down to The Bay for light snacks, healthy salads, and a cocktail or two. As the day comes to a close, Bommu Rooftop Bar is the perfect place to be, offering stunning views and a buzzing social vibe.


    Fusion

    Fusion offers guests an all-day dining experience. Enjoy breakfast, lunch, or dinner overlooking the pool and terrace area and watch our team do what we do best with our open kitchen and action stations. Fusion serves up international cuisine and Asian favorites, inspired by Asian street food

    Opening hours

    Daily 6:30 am - 10:30 pm
