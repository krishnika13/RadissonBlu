Host your next meeting or event at Radisson Blu Resort, Galle

Radisson Blu Resort Galle, is ready and prepared for your next meeting and event. With four meeting spaces to choose from with varying capacities, we can cater to most requests from small intimate business conferences to large glamorous gala dinners. All our meeting rooms are equipped with state-of-the-art technology, such as sound systems and free Wi-Fi, to ensure your event runs smoothly.

Meetings & Events Facilities

With four meetings rooms available, this versatile space can cater to any meeting or event. Our dedicated and enthusiastic team will ensure your event runs smoothly and to your satisfaction. Our Grand Ballroom is the largest venue with a maximum capacity of 400 guests, perfect for large events or gala dinners. The other three meeting rooms are much more intimate, ideal for business meetings. All our rooms are state-of-the-art technology such as a sound system and free Wi-Fi for your convenience.
