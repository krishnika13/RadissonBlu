    Yala National Park


    As a gateway to the mesmerizing wilderness, we invite our guests to explore the captivating landscapes of Yala during the day, where they can embark on exhilarating safaris and encounters with the park's iconic wildlife. With expert guides, guests can witness the untamed beauty of nature up close, creating lasting memories of adventure.

    After an action-packed day, the hotel offers a sanctuary of comfort and relaxation, allowing travelers to unwind in style, dine on exquisite cuisine, and relive their outdoor escapades

    sunset cruises


    Embarking on a sunrise cruise at Radisson Blu Resort promises a breathtaking adventure like no other. As the first rays of dawn kiss the tranquil waters surrounding the resort, guests are treated to a mesmerizing spectacle of nature's beauty.

    The added touch of luxury and comfort that Radisson Blu Resort provides, from expertly guided cruises to gourmet breakfasts served on board, elevates the sunrise cruise into an unforgettable adventure that embodies both relaxation and wonder.

    Whale watching


    Embarking on a whale-watching adventure at Radisson Blu Resort is an unparalleled experience that combines the luxury of a beachfront getaway with the thrill of witnessing nature's giants up close

    Guests can set sail in comfort, guided by knowledgeable experts who lead the expedition to the best whale-watching spots. As the resort caters to both relaxation and adventure, travelers can start their day with a gourmet breakfast before venturing out to sea in search of these magnificent creatures.


    LOCATION:

    Surfing


    Surfing at Radisson Blu Resort is an exhilarating adventure that perfectly balances the thrill of riding the waves with the luxury and comfort of a beachfront retreat. With its idyllic coastal location, the resort serves as an ideal base for surf enthusiasts of all levels.

    Beginners can take advantage of expert surf instructors who offer lessons tailored to individual skills and preferences. For experienced surfers, the nearby breaks provide an exciting challenge. The thrill of catching the perfect wave and riding it to shore is an experience like no other.
